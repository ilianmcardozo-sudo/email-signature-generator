How to add it to Gmail

  1. Fill in your details and click Copy signature.
  2. In Gmail, go to ⚙ Settings → See all settings.
  3. Scroll to Signature and create a new one (or edit the existing one).
  4. Click inside the signature box and paste with Ctrl + V (Cmd + V on Mac).
  5. Scroll to the bottom and click Save changes.
About the photo: Gmail caps signatures at about 10,000 characters, so your photo is automatically resized to 100×100 and compressed to fit. The counter above the preview shows how much room is left. If Gmail still drops the photo when you save, use the Insert image button in Gmail's signature editor and drag your photo into the empty space on the left.
